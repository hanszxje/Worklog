> Feel free to share your thoughts and feedback regarding your journey with the First Cloud Journey program. Your input is vital in helping the FCJ team refine future cohorts across these areas:

### General Assessment of the Internship

**1. Work Environment**  
The atmosphere here is exceptionally welcoming, collaborative, and friendly. Team members are always ready to assist whenever I run into technical challenges, even beyond standard working hours. The office space is clean, modern, and highly conducive to productivity. I recommend organizing minor social events or team-building activities to help interns connect better.

**2. Mentor & Admin Support**  
My mentor was outstanding—providing structured guidance, explaining complex topics clearly, and encouraging continuous questioning. The administrative staff was also highly responsive in handling paperwork and provisioning resources. What I valued most was how my mentor encouraged self-guided problem solving before suggesting solutions.

**3. Alignment with Field of Study**  
The assignments aligned perfectly with my university coursework while introducing me to modern cloud concepts. This dual benefit allowed me to apply theoretical knowledge and acquire new practical skills simultaneously.

**4. Learning & Professional Development**  
This internship has significantly enhanced my technical capabilities and soft skills. I gained hands-on experience using industry-standard project management tools, collaborating in teams, and communicating professionally in a corporate setting. The career advice shared by my mentor also helped clarify my future professional path.

**5. Workplace Culture & Team Collaboration**  
The organizational culture is highly positive and inclusive. Everyone works with dedication while maintaining a supportive and friendly mood. During peak project phases, the mutual support across teams was prominent, making me feel valued and integrated as part of the team.

**6. Benefits & Internship Policies**  
The company offers a reasonable stipend and allows flexible working hours to accommodate academic schedules. Additionally, having access to internal technical training sessions was a great advantage.

### Survey Questions

* **What did you enjoy most about your internship?**  
  Collaborating directly with experienced Solution Architects at AWS, utilizing sandbox accounts to build infrastructure (with EC2, RDS, Glue, and Lambda), and successfully implementing an end-to-end data pipeline integrated with a machine learning forecasting model.
* **What improvements would you suggest for future interns?**  
  Conducting technical workshops on cloud cost optimization (FinOps), system debugging (troubleshooting), and cloud security early in the program to help new interns onboard more efficiently.
* **Would you recommend this internship to others? Why?**  
  Yes, without hesitation. AWS Vietnam offers an excellent learning environment with challenging but rewarding projects, dedicated mentorship, and early exposure to cloud technologies that will benefit anyone's career.

### Recommendations & Future Goals

* **Any suggestions to improve the overall internship experience?**  
  I recommend hosting a "Demo Day" at the conclusion of the internship so all First Cloud Journey teams can present their work, exchange ideas, and get feedback from senior AWS leaders.
* **Would you be interested in continuing with this program in the future?**  
  Definitely. I look forward to participating in AWS developer communities or pursuing full-time employment opportunities at AWS after graduation.
* **Additional Comments:**  
  I would like to express my sincere gratitude to the FCJ organizing team, the mentors, and my fellow interns for making this a highly rewarding and memorable experience.
